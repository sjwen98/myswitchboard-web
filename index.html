Features

Built for people who manage more than one dashboard

Every feature exists to cut one specific piece of tedium out of logging into the same sites, over and over.

Batch login tabs

Pick your sites and mySwitchBoard opens each one as a real WebView tab, queued and run one at a time — not a wall of windows.

Visual element picker

No CSS selectors to write by hand. Tap the real username, password, or submit button on the page — the app works out the selector for you.

Flows: record & replay

Record a sequence of clicks and inputs once — a report you pull every week, a filter you always set — and replay it in one tap.

Premium

Face ID / fingerprint lock

An extra biometric gate in front of your account, so a signed-in phone isn't the same thing as an unlocked one.

Vault-grade credentials

Every password is encrypted at rest in Supabase Vault and only ever exists in plaintext for the instant it's typed into a login form.

Sign in your way

Email and password, or Google, Facebook, and X — pick whichever account you already trust.

How it works

Set it up once, run it every day

1

Add your sites

Give each dashboard a name, its login URL, and your credentials — encrypted straight into Supabase Vault.

2

Point at the fields

Tap the login page's username, password, and submit button once. mySwitchBoard remembers the selectors.

3

Log in to everything

Select your sites and hit go — each one opens, fills in, and signs in, one tab after another.

Your passwords never sit on the device

Site credentials are stored server-side in Supabase Vault, decrypted just-in-time by a locked-down edge function for the moment a login form actually needs them, and never persisted into local app state. Combined with an optional biometric app lock, the app is built so a lost or unlocked phone still isn't a leaked password vault.
